Jumbo Homes Loans Up to $10 Million with NexGen Lending

The demand for higher end housing is booming. And that means buyers need jumbo financing. At NexGen Lending, a division of NexGen Lending, we have just the right financing at competitive rates. Most jumbo lenders, either here in the Greater Boise area or even online lenders with a national presence limit how high their jumbo loans can be and set limits on them. Further, such loans can have larger down payment requirements and require higher rates than what we can provide.

Our jumbo loan limit is $10 million. And beyond that, we’re from here. We know the real estate financing market like no one else and provide an array of jumbo and “super” jumbo loan options from fixed rate to adjustable rate to hybrid choices.
